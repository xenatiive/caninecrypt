TERMS OF SERVICE

  • All commissions will ONLY be accepted through either Paypal or Ko-fi and in USD.
  • At least 50% of the full payment must be received before I begin working on the commission. Until the full payment is received, any progress photos will be delivered to the commissioner with a watermark.
  • References are REQUIRED -- visual references are preferred and clearer, but in-depth text can work as well.
  • Commissions for commercial purposes are x2 the regular price! Must be discussed prior to payment.
  • Chances are, I will publicly share the commission work I do. Please specify prior to payment if you'd like to opt out and keep it as a private commission.
  • Understand that I have both academic and profession-related obligations that may effect the rate at which I draw.
  • Understand that due to specific communication needs, I require clear and concise communication and instruction.
  • I have the right to decline a commission for any reason.
  • No refunds once a sketch has been proofed!

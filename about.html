All about the artist...

Queer    Neurodivergent    Indigenous    he/him    19

Hi! I'm Crow and I've been doing art ever since I could pick up and use a pencil. It was over for me as soon as I discovered that I had a specific passion towards drawing animals and anything that was basically not a human (though I still do enjoy drawing those too on occasion ;3c).

I primarily draw on an iPad 9 and with Procreate, and I draw what I feel like drawing, and whatever makes me happy.

  • I love all things fantasy

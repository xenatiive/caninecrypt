Mystery Commissions

Pay what you want and get something for the same value in return! The commissioner is welcome and encouraged to provide details and specific requests that will be included in the piece, but the artist will have free creative liberty with your character(s) and the chosen medium! This is likely to result in more experimental pieces.

There is a minimum payment of $30 USD, and a cap at $250 USD. As always, discuss with the artist prior to payment.
